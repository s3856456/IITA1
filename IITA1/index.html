Project Idea

Overview

A smart application that generates delicious and nutritious recipes for consumer desires. Within the app there will be various functions and usages such as evaluating the nutritional values of various foods and ingredients which creates recipes that strives to help users that will cater to achieve their nutritional and dietary goals. The end user will be provided a daily report which showcases the amount of nutrition consumed per day such as, calorie intake, carbs, protein, and fat etc.

Motivation

The attaching image showcases on a chart the percentage of U.S. adults who would use an app to track their daily nutritional intake and diet which was conducted via a survey from 962 participants in March 2017.

Statistics indicate that 26% of those aged 18 to 29 years use an app to track their diet regularly meaning out of the 962 people, roughly 250 people are in that category. Moreover, results show that 23% of those aged 30 to 45 years use them occasionally which is equivalent to 221 people out of the overall participants. This indicates that there are still a portion of the population who utilises similar apps that provide dietary and nutritional planning. Moreover, the software that I have chosen to develop provides the essentials that people already use, including many features that no develop has integrated into their app. Using the evidence shown in the statistics above, by incorporating additional features, consumers are more inclined to have an interest in my app and consequently utilising it for their own benefit.

Description

A personal dietitian for individuals seeking direction and guidance with meal preparations. Depending on the user’s goal (to lose fat, to gain muscle, maintain weight) the app will algorithmically generate a recipe encompassing a meal that satisfies the user’s needs and satisfies the nutritional needs of a person for each meal. With many apps currently available to download that provides a list of recipes for the users to prepare, this app differs from the other apps through additional features added that no other app has. This includes the ability for the software to generate recipes through garnering the pre-existing ingredients rather than collecting recipes online that may need some extra ingredients in-order to cook. This is a major step up as this app encompasses a dynamic algorithm which creates recipes based on ingredients already owned rather than spending extra on new ingredients thus benefiting users financially as they do not need to spend for every meal they prepare. This app also aims to ensure that we meet all user’s dietary requirements (vegans, vegetarian, food allergies and intolerances, religious reasons such as halal, etc) as health and safety including personal beliefs which should be considered as a top priority when it comes to designing an app related to foods. Hence, offering an additional menu that allows users to select and highlight what they can and cannot consume would benefit all end users. One key feature that this app provides is a daily report which highlights the amount of nutrition that is consumed each day which serves to promote a healthy diet as we can reflect on those results and make a positive change to reach our goal. An example to this may include tracking our calorie intake as this impacts our abilities to either lose or gain weight. By keeping a record of how many calories we consume each day we can distinguish whether we are reaching our goals such as a calorie surplus for putting on additional weight, or a calorie deficit for losing weight. In addition to this, if the daily goals are not yet reached by the end of the day, a notification will be sent to remind the user that they have either exceeded the recommended daily calorie intake, or they are well below the recommended daily intake. Another attribute is that there is an option that would allow the system to randomly generate a meal if we have not yet planned out our meals throughout the day. This is a useful tool for those who have trouble decided on what to cook throughout the day inclining them to likely skipping a meal which can result in tiredness as you are not providing your body with essential nutrients. This app is focuses mainly on being a user-friendly program allowing the user to navigate through the app with ease due to the quick and responsive feedback that this app delivers. For the first time launching this application, you will be asked to provide your height, weight, and age so that the system is able to calculate an accurate representation of what their specific body type is required to achieve a certain goal. However, it is not required if users do not feel comfortable sharing this information with the program.
